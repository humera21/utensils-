About Everything Kitchens

Who We Are

Everything Kitchens started in 2002 with a new mother wanting to work from home, with her first baby on the way. The owner, Emily Church, launched Everything Kitchens in her bedroom with $500 when she was 20 years old. Our company has grown tremendously over the years, now employing nearly 50 employees. Everything Kitchens currently occupies a spacious and modern headquarters (including a 50,000 sq. ft. warehouse!) in beautiful Springfield, Missouri.
Since our launch, Everything Kitchens has been committed to providing top-quality kitchenware and appliances at the lowest prices possible for customers, all while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and customer service. Each member of our friendly, knowledgeable sales staff has undergone extensive, hands-on training with the products we offer. We are so confident in our unbeatable pricing that we back each of our products with our Best-Price Guarantee up to seven (7) days after the sale.

What We Do

We recognize that our longevity as a growing business depends on the satisfaction of our loyal customers. With every interaction, we strive to provide the highest quality kitchenware and appliances at the most competitive pricing available.

Everything Kitchens was founded with a focus on helping others and making an impact in our community and around the world. Each year we support a limited number of local Southwest Missouri organizations through event assistance and fundraising auction donations. We proudly support World Vision, an international humanitarian organization serving the world's poor and displaced by providing programs which help save lives, bring hope, and restore dignity to these communities and their citizens. In addition to dollar-for-dollar matched employee payroll contributions, we donate a portion of the proceeds from every sale to World Vision to help those who need it most.
